　　

　　

　　工程描述：该项目合同额约2.56亿美元，业主为阿联酋迪拜交通局（RTA），设计监理单位为Wilbur Smith Associates。酋长路升级改造项目包括一段33公里长的高速路加宽，对现有的立交进行升级改造，和新建两座大型立交桥；同时包括道路标志牌、道路划线、路灯、雨水、污水、绿化、灌溉、交通导改等工程内容。该项目预计于2013年3月31日完工。

　　Scope of Work:

　　The proposed improvements involve widening of the existing highway of an approximate length of 33 km, and also include the upgrading of the existing intersection into grade separate interchange, in addition to the construction of two new interchanges .

　　The work also includes the construction of roadway signage and pavement marking, street lighting, storm drainage and sewerage, landscaping and irrigation system, diversion and/or protection of existing services and other new services.
